# Executive — public-audience drafts

Speeches, op-eds, interviews, investor narratives, public statements.

## In scope

- Public speeches and panel talks.
- Published op-eds or long-form essays.
- Interview preparation and media briefings.
- Investor update **narrative** (with numbers filled offline by Finance).
- Public policy statements.

## Out of scope

- Board materials.
- Investor updates with the numbers attached.
- M&A communications.
- Private partner commitments.
- Anything involving compensation or HR specifics.

## The cycle

### 1. CLARIFY
- Audience (investors / industry / regulators / general / developer).
- Venue / channel (conference, podcast, long-form publication, internal-external letter).
- Purpose (thought leadership, position statement, response, retrospective).
- Embargo / timing considerations.
- Tier: Restricted session → Internal artifact → Public on publish.
- Review ladder (Exec + Marketing + Legal for legal risk, Biz-Dev for partner-adjacent).

### 2. CAPTURE
**Nest** under Exec Lead role (or delegate). Restricted session; the artifact starts Internal.

Title: `[Talk / op-ed / interview] at [venue / for channel] delivered`

Acceptance criteria:
- Content draft in BSVA voice; no unreleased material.
- All factual claims cited from public sources.
- Review ladder completed (Marketing + Legal).
- Publish gate passed per `workflow/08-publish-gate.md`.

### 3. PLAN-MODE
Outline: opening, argument structure, evidence, close. Identify cites. Identify risks.

### 4. EXECUTE
Draft → self-check → Marketing review → Legal review (if risk) → Exec final → publish gate.

### 5. REPORT
Comment on nest: live URL, recording URL (if any), classification flipped, engagement metrics (if available).

### 6. CLOSE
Exec reviews acceptance criteria. Project Done.

## Voice

Exec in BSVA voice = calm, precise, grown-up, evidence-driven, neutral about competitors. Higher bar on voice than other depts because Exec's voice represents BSVA broadly.

## Hard rules

- **Never** quote named individuals without the subject's approval.
- **Never** characterize individuals' motives — describe actions and outcomes.
- **Never** speculate about BSV asset price, market cap, or speculative trajectory.
- **Never** promise what BSVA hasn't decided.
- **Never** publish an exec statement under time pressure without Legal sign-off if there's any legal risk.
