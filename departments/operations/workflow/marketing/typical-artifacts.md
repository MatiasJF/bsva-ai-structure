# Marketing — typical artifacts

| Rank | Artifact | Cadence | Default tier | Top skills |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Blog post / long-form content | Weekly | Internal → Public | `external-comm-check`, `classify-before-publish` |
| 2 | Social copy / campaign calendar | Daily | Internal → Public | `external-comm-check` |
| 3 | Event assets (landing, invite, follow-up) | Event-tied | Internal → Public | `event-pitch-drafter`, `external-comm-check` |
| 4 | One-pager / press release | Monthly | Internal → Public | `bsva-onepager`, `external-comm-check` |
| 5 | PR response / reactive comm | As needed | Internal → Public (usually) | `pr-response-drafter`, `external-comm-check` |

## The publish gate (Marketing version)

Marketing ships the most Public-tier content of any sub-section. The gate is applied rigorously:

- Accuracy — every factual claim citation-checked.
- Voice — `external-comm-check` passes.
- Classification flip — label change with comment.
- Attribution — no unannounced partners, no fabricated quotes, no AI imagery of real people.
- Approval — Marketing Lead + affected-department Lead for content touching their area.

## What's never in Marketing drafts

- Unannounced partner names (even as placeholders in final text — substitute generic descriptors like "a leading exchange partner").
- Internal commercial terms.
- Specific $ amounts tied to partners.
- Competitor attacks (technical comparisons OK; ad hominem not).
- Price / market speculation (BSVA voice rule).

## Coordination rhythms

- **Weekly content calendar review** — Marketing Lead + sub-owner per content stream. Nests tracked; tensions opened for dependencies (dev-review, design, partner clearance).
- **Campaign launch check** — 1-week-before and 1-day-before gate runs. Runs `classify-before-publish` both times.
- **Post-publish retro** — within 3 business days. Comments on the nest. Promotable learnings → skills.
