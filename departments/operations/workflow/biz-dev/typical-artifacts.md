## What's in vs out

**Fair game for biz-dev in Claude:**
- Public firm websites and blogs.
- Public LinkedIn / X posts.
- Published portfolios.
- Public interviews and conference appearances.
- Regulatory filings.

**Out of scope for biz-dev in Claude:**
- Private correspondence with partners (redact to placeholders first).
- Internal pipeline notes (what stage a deal is at, commercial terms).
- Draft or executed NDAs.
- Unannounced partner names paired with specific BSVA intent.
- Compensation / hiring / performance data.

## Classification flip

Biz-dev rarely publishes externally. The tier usually stays Confidential. When biz-dev content feeds into a public announcement (sponsorship reveal, partnership launch), the flip happens via Marketing — Biz-Dev tensions to Marketing with approved-for-release wording.
