# Business Development — CLAUDE.md

Extends `../CLAUDE.md` (operations) which extends the base. Security rules tighten here.

---

## Default classification

**Confidential.** Default-up: treat partnership content as Confidential unless explicitly marked otherwise.

Up-classify to Restricted when:
- A signed term sheet or contract is involved.
- An M&A candidate or transaction is involved.
- Revenue / commercial figures for a specific named partner are involved.

---

## What you'll be asked to help with

- Drafting partnership proposals (use placeholders).
- Drafting sponsorship asks (use placeholders).
- Summarizing a partner's public posture (public inputs only).
- Preparing for meetings (agenda, roles, open questions).

---

## Hard rules

- **Never** name an unannounced partner in Claude. Use `[PARTNER_A]` etc.
- **Never** paste draft contract language. Describe clause-level questions abstractly.
- **Never** paste partner email threads. Redact to placeholders; extract the question.
- **Never** paste specific commercial terms (amounts, percentages) together with the counterparty name.

## What IS fine

- Public statements by partners.
- Published BSVA content.
- Abstract questions about how partnerships typically work.
- Redacted drafts using only placeholders.

## Escalation

- Biz-Dev Lead — Nestr role: `Biz-Dev Lead`.
- Legal for anything contract-adjacent.
- Exec for strategic commitments.
