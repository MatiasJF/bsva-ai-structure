Education's motto

Accuracy over fluency. A confident wrong explanation is worse than a plain correct one. The fact-check-gate refusing to sign off on unsupported claims is a feature.

Hard lines for Education
  • Never paste identifiable learner submissions — anonymizer first.
  • Never process minors' data. Escalate to Education Lead + Legal.
  • Never publish a translation without native-speaker review.
  • Never make credential-affecting grading decisions — Claude drafts, humans sign.
