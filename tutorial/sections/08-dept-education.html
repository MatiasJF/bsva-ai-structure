Education playbook

You ship lessons, assessments, translations, learner-support replies, and course modules. Default tier: Internal drafts → Public on publish. Learner data: Confidential or Restricted (minors).

Artifact Cadence Top skills

Key skills (installed for Education)

  • lesson-outline-builder — enforces the pedagogy gate: audience, prerequisites, 3–5 verb-led objectives, assessment strategy. Refuses to expand without them.
  • quiz-generator — items whose answers quote the lesson verbatim; distractors keyed to plausible misconceptions.
  • reading-level-checker — Flesch-Kincaid + jargon density + simplification suggestions.
  • academy-term-linker — looks up every term via bsv_get_concepts; inserts canonical links.
  • learner-support-drafter — drafts replies with anonymizer auto-run. Never sends.

Education's motto

Accuracy over fluency

A confident wrong explanation is worse than a plain correct one. Lessons outlive their authors — they reach thousands of learners who can't easily evaluate our claims. The fact-check-gate refusing to sign off on unsupported claims is a feature.

Education-specific rules

  • Never paste identifiable learner submissions. Run anonymizer first.
  • Never process minors' data. Escalate to Education Lead + Legal.
  • Never publish a translation without native-speaker review.
  • Never make credential-affecting grading decisions — Claude drafts, humans sign.

Escalation owners

  • Learner-PII or minors — Education Lead + Legal (Restricted).
  • Technical accuracy — DevRel + Tech via tension.
  • Translation disputes — Education Lead.
