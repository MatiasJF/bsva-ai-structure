Operations playbook

Operations covers Biz-Dev, Marketing, Legal, and Exec. This is where content sensitivity peaks — defaults are stricter than any other department.

The Operations twist

Anonymize-first posture

The anonymizer skill auto-runs at the start of every Operations session. Unannounced partner names never enter Claude — they become [PARTNER_A]. Real values are filled in outside Claude.

Sub-section defaults

Biz-Dev
Default: Confidential

Lead lists, partner posture briefs, proposal shells (placeholders only).
Marketing
Default: Internal → Public

Blogs, social, events, press responses. Brand voice linter required.
Legal
Default: Restricted

Public research + generic templates only. BSVA-specific matter stays offline.
Exec
Default: Restricted

Public-audience drafts + public-source digests only. No board / M&A / HR.

Key skills (installed for Operations)

  • lead-list-builder — public-source lead lists with URL-cited rows (Biz-Dev).
  • vc-public-profile-researcher — single-target public posture (Biz-Dev).
  • market-maker-tracker — published positions over time (Biz-Dev).
  • partnership-proposal-shell — placeholder-only templates (Biz-Dev).
  • event-pitch-drafter — sponsor-facing event copy (Marketing).
  • pr-response-drafter — measured reactive comms (Marketing).
  • external-comm-check — BSVA voice linter (Marketing).
  • legal-public-research — public-law only (Legal).
  • exec-brief-builder — public-source exec digests (Exec).

Universal Operations hard lines

Never in any Claude session
  • Unannounced partner names — anywhere. Use [PARTNER_A].
  • Draft or executed contract language.
  • HR / compensation / performance / equity data.
  • Board minutes, memos, resolutions.
  • M&A, term sheets, due-diligence.
  • Commercial terms paired with real counterparty names.
  • AI imagery / fabricated quotes of real people.

Escalation owners

  • Biz-Dev — Biz-Dev Lead.
  • Marketing — Marketing Lead (+ Legal if risk, Exec if reputation).
  • Legal — Legal Lead. Any BSVA-specific matter stays offline.
  • Exec — Exec Lead.
  • Any Operations leak / incident — DevRel + Security immediately.
