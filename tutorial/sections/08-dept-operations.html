Hard lines across all of Operations
  • No unannounced partner names — anywhere. Use [PARTNER_A].
  • No draft or executed contract language.
  • No HR / compensation / performance / equity data.
  • No board minutes / memos / M&A / term sheets.
  • No commercial terms paired with real counterparty names.
  • No AI imagery or fabricated quotes of real people.
The Operations rule

Anonymize-first posture. The anonymizer skill runs at the start of every Operations session, before any user-pasted content is processed.
