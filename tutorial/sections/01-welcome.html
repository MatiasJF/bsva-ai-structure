What this repo is

The bsva-ai-structure repo gives every BSVA person the same starting point for using Claude: shared skills, shared rules, shared security practices, and department-specific templates. It's both a toolkit and a training program.

Who it's for

Everyone. Engineers, DevRel, educators, business development, marketing, legal, executive — each department has its own folder with its own workflow, but everyone starts with the same base. This tour walks you through that base.
