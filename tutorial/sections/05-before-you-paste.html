Before you paste

30 seconds of discipline that prevents weeks of cleanup. Claude will not stop you — you are the gate. Claude's refusal is only the last-resort backstop.

The 4-tier classification

Public Already published

bsvblockchain.org, published Academy, public GitHub, press releases, public on-chain data.

Internal BSVA-only, not published

Roadmaps, drafts, meeting notes, most Nestr tensions, internal RFCs.

Confidential Harm if leaked

Partner terms, unannounced features, unreleased financials, candidate pipeline.

Restricted Legal / fiduciary risk

Contracts, NDAs, board, M&A, HR, private keys. Never in Claude.

What Claude CAN and CAN'T do (myth-busting)

❌ Not true
Claude can read everything on my computer

It reads only what the session is permitted to read — usually the current project folder and files you attach.

❌ Not true
Claude remembers everything we ever talked about

Sessions are independent. Memory is file-based — you can read, edit, delete it.

❌ Not true
Claude is always online and searching the web

WebFetch / WebSearch are permissioned tools. Many sessions don't use either.

✅ True
You are accountable for what Claude does on your behalf

Your name is on the PR. Your signature is on the email. The classification is your call.

Hard refusals — non-negotiable
  • Private keys, seed phrases, API keys, production credentials. Never anywhere.
  • HR / salary / performance data. Route through HR process.
  • Board / M&A / contracts. Out of Claude entirely.
  • Unannounced partner names in drafts. Use placeholders.
