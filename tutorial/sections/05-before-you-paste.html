Your 30-second pre-flight

Is it already published or clearly public?
✓ Yes — Public. Go.
Keys · HR · legal · board · M&A?
Names · $ · NDA · unreleased?
✕ Yes — STOP. Escalate.
⚠ Redact → Confidential, scoped session
→ No — Internal. Desktop / CLI only.

What Claude CAN'T do (calm yourself)

Read any file on my disk
Only what you permit — usually current project folder and attached files.
Remember past conversations
Sessions are independent. Memory is plain files you can read and delete.
Always be online searching
WebFetch / WebSearch are permissioned tools. Many sessions use neither.
You are accountable for its output
Your name on the PR, your signature on the email. Your call on classification.
Hard refusals — non-negotiable
  • Private keys · seed phrases · API keys · production credentials. Nowhere, ever.
  • HR · salary · performance. Route through HR process.
  • Board · M&A · contracts. Out of Claude entirely.
  • Unannounced partners in drafts. Use [PARTNER_A].
