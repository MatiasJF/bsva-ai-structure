The spine of BSVA's Claude discipline. Every piece of shared work — from a 3-line dev-support reply to a month-long workshop — follows the same 10 steps.

Click any step above to see what it means.

When the cycle is mandatory

For shared / shipped work, always:

  • Anything that goes external (blog, PR, partner comm).
  • Anything that lands in a shared BSVA system.
  • Anything that takes more than 30 minutes or touches more than 2 files.
  • Anything that requires another role's review.

When you can skip

  • Throwaway prototypes, personal notes, one-shot research.
  • Trivial transformations (format text, convert JSON → YAML).
  • 3-line fixes with an obvious outcome.

The distinction: does the output have an audience beyond you?
